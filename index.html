Field notes

Polyglots Immigrant Services is a nonprofit based in Niagara Falls, ON, dedicated to assisting immigrants with day-to-day life skills. We help newcomers navigate Canadian systems, build confidence, and connect with their new community.

Our work is grounded in practical support: from settlement guidance to life skills workshops, volunteer opportunities, and community donations.

Support paths

Settlement Support

Guidance on housing, healthcare, and community resources to help you settle in Niagara Falls.

Life Skills Workshops

Practical sessions on budgeting, transportation, and Canadian customs.
